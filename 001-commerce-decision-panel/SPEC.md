# Commerce Decision Panel
Version 0.1

## Purpose

Help customers make purchasing decisions faster without leaving the product page.

---

## Problem

When products have many options, customers must repeatedly click different variants to compare them.

This creates friction and increases decision fatigue.

---

## Solution

Display a persistent information panel beside the product options.

Customers can instantly preview important information before selecting.

---

## Core Features

### Live Preview

Hover an option to preview information.

---

### Product Image

Large preview image.

---

### Specifications

Dimensions

---

### Material

Primary materials.

---

### Reference Price

Display MSRP or comparison price.

---

### Description

Short marketing description.

---

## Responsive

Desktop:
Right-side sticky panel.

Tablet:
Panel below options.

Mobile:
Accordion or bottom drawer.

---

## Performance

No page reload.

No app dependency.

Works inside Shopify themes.

Minimal JavaScript.
